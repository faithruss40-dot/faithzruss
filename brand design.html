Inferno Wings & Things

These mockups were created using Adobe software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. These photos showcase the branding for Inferno Wings & Things,a fake hot wings restaurant. Each mockup has consistent colors and design. The logo is an emo roaster with flames behind it, representing alternative flavors of wings and other food the restaurant would serve.
