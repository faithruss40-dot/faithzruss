Photo Illustrations

Project: Photo to Digital Painting

This artwork was created by converting a photo into a painting. It was based on a photo of a beautiful woman in a kimono. Through the use of Photoshop, it was transformed from a photo into an artwork. It utilizes brushwork to give the image texture and drawing quality. The final project is a beautiful example of mixing photography and art together.

Project: Photo to Digital Illustration

This artwork was created by staging a photo and remaking it into vector artwork. There was a careful arrangement of light and props to build an interesting base for the illustration. The recreation in Adobe Illustrator utilized multiple layers and careful tracing to bring the photo to life. After many hours of work, this truly unique and complex illustration was born.
